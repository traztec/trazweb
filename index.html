Who are we ?

Founded in Singapore in 2014, Traztec Pte Ltd is a privately held General Construction firm specializing in scaffolding and access works for commercial, retail, office and interior construction projects.

What we do ?

Since its inception, Traztec Pte Ltd has earned a sound reputation with clients for providing a high level of technical scaffold services at competitive prices.

Scaffold Consultancy

Supervision and installation of metal scaffolding and other related access solution at construction sites

Scaffold Erection and Dismantling

Our team are trained and experienced in carrying out scaffolding erection and dismantling operations

Rope Access Services

Our highly trained personnel specialize in inspecting, maintenance, cleaning and repair services at great heights

Commercial Cleaning

Traztec offers a range of facade and high-rise cleaning services for commercial buildings and residential homes too.
